7 Challenges that inspired us

Back In 2016 when I requested for my Adsense pin to be delivered to me at a location in South-Eastern part of Nigeria (Oko, Orumba South, Anambra state precisely) after submitting the address details I wasn’t able to get this PIN from Google, I have to resubmit again with a different address location so I could be able to get the pin delivered to me, the problem here is that the first address I used was difficult to locate, so I have to use a popular address which I requested permission before using it.

The African Race is a developing people and environs equipped with diverse resources, but yet still strained by limitation and yet to be explored opportunities, despite the impressive invasion of science and technology. Africa is yet to fully come to the knowledge, understand or even harness her virtues and these have become great array of difficulties to the Africa souls. These problems are booming with strength, keeping us at a siege, leaving a large part of the Africa populace hopeless and helpless to their sting. Nevertheless, we found pleasure in stepping into the mire of these difficulties and want to bring out hope in them, because these problems sponsored and inspired us to innovation, as popularly said "necessity is the mother of invention" Such Problems are:

1. Dis-Jointed communication and network between the Local and Urban regions:  It has been observes that there is great division and dis-joint between the Urban regions and the Rural regions due to distance, road network and other constraints. This circumstance have brought about great set-back to the development of rural regions, limiting their exploiting great values and virtues and becoming part of the trends of science and technology that is common in the urban region(s).

2. Covid-19 Pandemic resurgence and doggedness and other cases similar: During the Covid-19 pandemic invasion in the year 2020, a major part of the world were crippled because there was no measure to fall back to. In addition the Covid-19 Pandemic is barely one year extincted or cubed, but it has resurfaced and fighting hard because the whole protective measures have been lowered and many have found their regular unhealthy and unproductive lives wheeling again, simply because there seem to be no other means to live other than the conventional way of life that helped the virus spread earlier.

3. High Cost of Transporting goods and individuals alike across the state and beyond the nation: The increase in gasoline and other automobile motor engines propellant have directly affected the cost of transporting of individuals and goods, bringing it to the height of economic digression, increasing the cost of living. This problem affects productivity and reduces output.

4. Unemployment and limitations to resources for Job creation: The rate of unemployment within the Nigeria Economical setting and across Africa is no longer a strange wind, having so many graduates and undergraduates alike without any meaningful financial engagement is now a threat to both lives and economy of many. Unemployment is the parent to many crime and economic limitation. In addition to the fact that many other may want to be responsible and legally engaged, the resources are far-fetched.

5. Dependency on open market: The obvious dependency on open market system was proven to be a danger in the year 2020, when the world's peace was threatened by the deadly Covid-19 virus invasion, this worldwide catastrophe led to a nation wide declared lock-down across the globe. In the light of this, many businesses shut down, feeding became almost a threat and so many other activities were abated because of complete dependency on open market. There were records of increased crimes, death and starvation, simply because the open market was shut down.

6. Failure of organizations to build a sustainable Errand and Delivery services: Over the years, several companies have come up with ideas to build and as well developed a sustainable Errand and Delivery service that is owned and managed by Africans, Nigerians in particular, which has failed woefully over a period of time. This issue has eaten deep into several life savings and killed many financial homes. The inability to build a sustainable online based Delivery platform in Africa is almost a plaque.

7. Inability to create Multiple stream of Productivity: Several individuals have desire to create multi-source of income, expansion and productivity but the tedious engagement with their current business and tight schedule have kept that at an impossible height. Not minding the fact that the current job is not paying and aiding as much as desired or as it promises to. For this stance, many passionate persons have been kept at an economic stagnation and limitation. In additin Many wants to be productive and achieve alot within the space of 24hrs that is given to man, but have been limited by other circumstances on the other hand. After a series of research and analysis on delivery services in Nigeria, I and my team decided to start working on a startup that will solve major delivery issues in Nigeria and Africa as a whole.

Our Solution

Solving the problem of locating an address is trivial in the Nigerian delivery system also not just locating the address but locating it on time, Here is what our project proposes: Solving Delivery Nightmare in Nigeria with Mobile and Offline Technologies, we want to create a delivery service you can trust for fast and slick delivery. The system we have to inject into the Economic and Social atmosphere is a special approach system designed specifically to defeat the strength of these challenges named and those that may arise subsequently as the case maybe overtime. We are bringing in a structure to the Economic and Socio-Econimic system already existing in Nigeria and Africa, to serve as an aid, means of strong-hold and redefinition. Our approach is to inject help into the system, in a bid to do that we are bringing these:

1. Multi Phased Delivery and Errand Scheme: This is our first approach designed to address the afore listed challenges. As part of our desire to produce a formidable delivery scheme as well as errand system, we are putting in place a delivery system that will allow customers to be able to place order and get their goods picked up from any location, give room for customer-company vetting, delivered goods checking, tracking of their stuffs, connecting to different stores. As well as delivery and pick up of internationally delivered goods, goods for export, goods for local distribution etc. Our platform gives customers the avenue to do so much, beyond pick ups, they can send invitations, send for errands while thy comfortably continue with their normal life.

2. Inter/Intra Regional Access Point: On this note, we are offering customers the trust and ease we promised by giving them a neighborhood liaison centers and partners where they can communicate with, pick up their goods, and lay complaints. These liaison centers, pick-up points and partner bodies/organizations have direct connections to every other like centers across the nations which help to build customer's trust and improve excellent services. This Regional Access Points will be full representative of the organization.

3. Employing over 100 staffs:  This platform will create job opportunity to over hundred(100) persons irrespective of academic background and qualifications. There will be courier service, errand marchant, office managers, business administrators, drivers and so on. Our delivery platform will help reduce un-employment at first the local government level, then forge ahead to the state level and beyond.

4. Engage From Your Location System: A rare feature in a regular delivery or errand system, where our customers can place order, speak to customer agents, connect with a courier agent, track his/her goods, lay complaints, make enquiries, make payments and so on from any location without having to go to any physical shop or location. Our customers will have the avenue to engage with all the registered shops, and outlet for better services from any location to any location.

5. Multi- Registered Outlet and Open Market online We are creating a system that translates the experience, feeling and possibilities of an open market to an online system. Whatever a customer can do in an open market we are giving almost 100% replica of that experience. On our platform, our customers have the avenue to connect with different stores, open markets, and errand agents simultaneously with ease. These services are available within a short time and with the best result.

6. Subsidized Transition Cost: We have a special approach to the cost of transporting goods across the nation, despite the price in transportation, our platform is designed to be minimal in charging for transition of goods, pick-ups, errands etc. Our approach will be considerate and effectful while we put to work on reaching out to our customers with as little profitable cost as possible.

7. Rural Connection: One of our major special feature, we are bringing a system that will not only benefit the urban dwellers or functional within the city axis, but efficient within the rural areas also. On this platform customers from rural regions can make order for pick-up in the city and have it delivered to them right in that village or community. On our platform every customer is connected to the world and the global community.
